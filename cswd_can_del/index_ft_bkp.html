10% of overall marks are awarded for lab work (1% per week in the first 10 weeks).

  • Instructions for how to upload your work are given in the first two labs, named Create GitHub Repository and Use GitHub Repository.
  • Lab assignments are always due on Wednesday, just before the lab period starts.
  • Lab assignment labels in the table:
    • [C] denotes an assignment that must be completed but is not marked
    • [M] denotes an assignment that will be taken into account for lab marks
    • [P] denotes an assignment for additional practice
