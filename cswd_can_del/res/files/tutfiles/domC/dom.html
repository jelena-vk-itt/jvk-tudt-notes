Information about animals

Dogs are friendly and loyal to their owners.

Cats are independent and are said to have seven lives.

Rabbits are cute but you have to be careful not to step on them.

Hamsters run around in hamster wheels and can be vicious.

Tortoises are slow and steady and will often outlive their owners.

Snakes are interesting pets, if that is the word.
